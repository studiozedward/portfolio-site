Commbini

Strategic communications tool · 2026

Commbini is an AI-powered strategic communications planning platform built around the OASIS framework — a structured methodology covering Objectives, Audience, Strategy, Implementation, and Scoring. It is designed for communications professionals who need to move from a brief to a fully-formed campaign plan without losing strategic rigour.

The platform combines a curated knowledge base of communications research, case studies, and market intelligence with Claude to generate context-grounded outputs at each stage of the planning process.

Tools

- Brief Analyser — Reviews campaign briefs across all OASIS dimensions, scores quality, identifies gaps, and generates stakeholder summaries including email briefs and meeting notes

- OASIS Planner — Builds a complete campaign plan through six guided stages with knowledge-base context, coherence review, and iterative refinement; exports as a formatted document

- Implementation Planner — Converts an OASIS plan into a detailed workstream structure with task assignments, dependencies, and timing; generates a downloadable Gantt chart
